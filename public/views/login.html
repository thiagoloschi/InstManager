InstManager is a Node.js based web application, created with the purpose of providing Instagram users with cool features and insights from your favorite photo app. Get to know which of your friends were most tagged on your pictures, and what tags and locations you've used the most. You will also be able to manage your relationships to other users. See who doesn't follow you back and who you don't follow back. It's never been so easy to manage your Instagram Account. New features are being developed and will be available soon.
